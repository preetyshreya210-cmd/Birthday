Letters Waiting for You

These are not plain messages. These are paper pieces of my heart.

Open when you miss me

No matter where you are, a part of my heart is always with you. Distance can never really change that.

Open when you feel low

You are still soft, still special, still enough — even on the days that feel heavy.

Open when you need motivation

I hope you never forget how capable you are. You have already come so far.

Open when you want to smile

Think of our silly little moments and know there are so many more waiting for us.

Open when you need love

You are loved on your best days, tired days, quiet days, and every in-between day too.

Open when you doubt yourself

You are already enough. You do not have to earn the love that is already yours.
